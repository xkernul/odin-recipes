Odin Recipes

Listing of ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs of boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 2 blocks of cream cheese
  • 1 stick of butter
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 4 cloves or garlic
  • 6 oz of shredded parmeson cheese
  • ground pepper

Preperation Steps

  1. Beging frying Chicken in frying pan,cook and flip until done
  2. Boil Water for pasta, cook until done. Choose how you prefernce your noods.
  3. Strain Pasta, set aside.
  4. Melt butter down in sauce pan.
  5. Cube cream cheese, and whisk into melted butter.
  6. Slowly add 2 cups of milk while whisking
  7. Add in seasonings(garlic, ground pepper>
  8. Add Shredded parmeson cheese, continue whisking until smooth.
  9. Cude or dice chicken
