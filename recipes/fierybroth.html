Odin Recipes: Red Dragon Inn Fiery Broth

Listing of ingredients

  • 1 Onion
  • 3 Garlic Cloves
  • 2 celery stocks
  • 1/2 oz fresh ginger root
  • 1 3/4 oz coconut milk
  • 2pt chicken broth
  • 1 small tin tomato paste
  • 1 T curry powder
  • 4 cornichons (baby pickeled cucumber
  • salt and pepper
  • Tabasco sauce, to taste

Preperation Steps

  1. Peel the onion, garlic, celery, and ginger.Cut them into large put them in a sauce pan, to which you will add the coconut milk, chicken broth, tomato paster, curry powder, and cornichons.
  2. Mixtogether well and bring to a boil. Simmer and reduce for about 45 minutes. Taste and season.
  3. Strain soup through a strainer, then through a cloth to obtain a very clear broth. Taste, adjust the seasoning if necessary, and add Tabasco sauce to taste
  4. Serve steaming hot.
