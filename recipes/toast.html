Odin Recipes: Beorn's Goat Cheese Toast!

Listing of Ingredients

  • 4 small or 2 large goat cheese logs
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 1 pinch freshly grated nutmeg
  • 4 slices of bread
  • 1 handful fresh herbs(basil,marjoram,thyme)
  • 4 T honey
  • 1 seasonal lettuce

Preperation Steps

  1. Using a very sharp blade, cut each small cheese in half lengthwwise, or each large cheese into slices 1/2 inch thick.
  2. Season with salt and pepper, sprinkle lightly with nutmeg, and lay the pieces of cheese on the bread slices.
  3. Brab your herbs, rinse, drain, and chop them finely. Sprinke the slices with fresh herbs and then drizzle with honey.
  4. Place them under a broiler for about 5 minutes, until the cheese melts, the honey caramelizes, and the bread becomes crusty. Serve with seasonal salad.
